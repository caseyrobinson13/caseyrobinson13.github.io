About Us

Welcome to KC Coffee, where every cup is a blend of comfort, quality, and creativity. Our story begins with a love for exceptional coffee and a desire to share that experience with you. Located in San Luis Obispo, KC Coffee is more than just a place to grab a drink—it’s a community hub where coffee, conversation, and connection come together.

At KC Coffee, we believe that coffee should be an experience, not just a caffeine fix. That’s why we work with local roasters and carefully source the finest beans to create rich, flavorful brews that are always fresh and full of character. Whether you're here for an espresso shot to fuel your day, a perfectly crafted latte, or a refreshing iced drink, our menu is designed to offer something for every coffee lover.

But it’s not just about the coffee—it’s about the people. We’ve built KC Coffee to be a space where you can slow down, unwind, and enjoy a sense of community. Whether you’re working remotely, meeting a friend, or just taking a moment to yourself, we aim to make every visit feel like a moment of comfort and joy.

Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, handcrafted coffee while fostering a warm, inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome. We strive to bring out the best in every cup, and we believe that the perfect coffee experience goes hand-in-hand with great service and a space that feels like home.
